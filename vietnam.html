Snaps from Vietnam

I've been around Vietnam for a while, perhaps the most diverse place I've ever been. In these images you will find the chaos of Hanoi, tombs of emperors, rice fields in the north of the country, streets full of lanterns in Hoi An, Ha-Long Bay, nights in Saigon and more. You can open each one separately to see it in full resolution. Shoot with Halide on an iPhone and edited with Pixelmator Photo on an iPad.
